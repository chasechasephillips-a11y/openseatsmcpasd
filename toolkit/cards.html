Business cards — print sheet

10 cards per letter sheet, sized for Avery 5371 or any 3.5"×2" card stock. Print on cardstock (65 lb or heavier), then cut along the dashed lines. The cut marks won't print as solid lines — they're just a visual guide on screen.

In the print dialog: Letter · Portrait · Margins: None · Background graphics: ON.
