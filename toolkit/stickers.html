Stickers — print sheet (Avery 22806 layout)

12 round 2.5" stickers per letter sheet, 6 barn-red + 6 cream. For print at home: use Avery 22806 sticker paper, or use full sticker paper and cut with a 2.5" circle punch. For pro quality (vinyl, weatherproof, fade-resistant), upload this saved-as-PDF to one of the vendors in the printing guide — Sticker Mule, StickerYou, or VistaPrint.

In the print dialog: Letter · Portrait · Margins: None · Background graphics: ON. Sticker paper aligns with the 0.75" sheet margins built into the layout.
