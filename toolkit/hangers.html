Door hangers — print sheet

Three hangers per letter sheet, 4.25" × 3.33" each. Print on cardstock (65 lb+), cut along the dashed lines, and use a 7/16" hole punch on the red circle at the top to make the doorknob loop. Then loop the hanger over the doorknob and slit downward from the hole to the edge.

In the print dialog: Letter · Portrait · Margins: None · Background graphics: ON.
