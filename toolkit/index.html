Campaign toolkit

Help spread the word.

Three things, all free, all yours. Print the cards to leave in coffee shops and slip into birthday-party goodie bags. Tape the flyer to community bulletin boards. Paste the social copy when you don't know what to say.

01

Business cards

10 cards per letter sheet — Avery 5371 layout. Print on cardstock, cut along the lines, carry a dozen with you. URL + QR code on every card.

Open print sheet
02

Half-sheet flyer

Two flyers per letter, fold or cut in half. Big "OPEN THE SEATS" headline, three facts, the dates, a QR. Tape it up at the library, Capital Brewery, your church bulletin board.

Open print sheet
03

Door hangers

Three 4.25"×3.33" hangers per letter sheet, with a punch-hole template for the doorknob loop. Walk a block, hang on doorknobs, no doorbell required.

Open print sheet
04

Placard + circulator script

Page 1: 5.5"×8.5" tabletop placard with QR (for coffee shop / farmers market / library). Page 2: a circulator cheat sheet — the 30-second pitch, common objections + answers, what to bring.

Open print sheet
05

Social share kit

Pre-written copy for Facebook, NextDoor, a text to a neighbor, an email to a PTA. Plus three square graphics you can screenshot and post.

Open share kit
Printing tips. On the cards and flyer pages, hit Ctrl/Cmd + P → choose Save as PDF if you want to take it to FedEx Office, or print directly if you have cardstock at home. Use Letter size, portrait orientation, and turn on Background graphics in More Settings so the cream paper and red accents come through.
