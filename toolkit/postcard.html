Town of Middleton postcard

One 6.5"×9" postcard per page, two pages (front + back), sized to exactly 9 × 6.5 inches so the PDF uploads clean to any mailer. 6.5×9 is the smallest USPS-eligible size — a true 6×9 gets rejected (height must exceed 6.125"). Save as PDF and upload to a direct-mail service, or home-print and trim.

Upload to a mailer site (e.g., Vistaprint, Got Print, PostcardMania, or USPS EDDM):

  • Click "Print or save as PDF" below and choose Save as PDF. You'll get a two-page PDF — page 1 is the front design, page 2 is the address side.
  • At the mailer site, pick the 6.5" × 9" postcard template (or "6×9 EDDM flat" for door-direct-mail by carrier route — no addresses needed, USPS handles it).
  • Upload front + back as separate sides. Most services have a fit/crop step.
  • For EDDM specifically, the dashed indicia box is a placeholder — the EDDM tool replaces it automatically.

Print at home: Letter paper, Landscape, choose Print backgrounds in More Settings so the cream and brass come through. Cut along the corner crops.

QR code: auto-generated via api.qrserver.com — needs an internet connection at print time so the QR renders into the PDF.
